Sales Execution Playbook

The non-negotiable 6-step process and universal objection handling.

The 6-Step Process

Identify your door opener Always target HR: Head of HR, HR Director, Chief People Officer, or Head of Employee Benefits. Do not go to the CEO first.
Make first contact Match the culture. Shell needs a formal letter; Flutterwave needs a Linkedin DM. Mismatching culture signals incompetence.
Send a teaser before the meeting WhatsApp or email a one-page summary. Maximum 5 bullet points. No long PDFs.
Present the dual pitch (30 mins) Lead with the Staff Offer (equity installments + cash refund + appliances). This creates excitement.
Leave a commitment form (EOI) Do not leave without placing a physical or digital Expression of Interest form. It collects name, phone, monthly income bracket, and interest level to get 10 names.
Follow up within 48 hours Offer to run an in-office Staff Lunch Briefing — a 45-minute session where you present directly to eligible staff.

Universal Objection Handling

"We already have a mortgage scheme."
This is not a competing product it is a housing benefit layer. The cash refund and appliance grant sit on top of any existing scheme.
"How do we know the properties are legitimate?"
Full documentation: Certificate of Occupancy, development approvals, offtake agreements, and references from institutional partners. Site visits available.
"This sounds too good to be true."
"The cash refund is funded from the bulk offtake discount our developer extends to us for bringing 10+ buyers at once". The appliance grant comes out of the same pool.
