Sales Execution Playbook

The non-negotiable 6-step process and universal objection handling.

The 6-Step Process

1
Identify your door opener
Always target HR: Head of HR, HR Director, Chief People Officer, or Head of Employee Benefits. Do not go to the CEO first.
2
Make first contact
Match the culture. Shell needs a formal letter; Flutterwave needs a Linkedin DM. Mismatching culture signals incompetence.
3
Send a teaser before meeting
WhatsApp or email a one-page summary. Maximum 5 bullet points. No long PDFs.
4
Present the dual pitch
Keep it to 30 mins. Lead with the Staff Offer (equity installments + cash refund + appliances). This creates immediate excitement.
5
Leave a commitment form
Do not leave without placing an EOI form. It collects name, phone, monthly income bracket, and interest level to get 10 names.
6
Follow up within 48 hours
Offer to run an in-office Staff Lunch Briefing — a 45-minute session where you present directly to eligible staff.

Universal Objections

?
"We already have a mortgage scheme."
This is not a competing product it is a housing benefit layer. The cash refund and appliance grant sit on top of any existing scheme.
?
"How do we know the properties are legitimate?"
Full documentation: Certificate of Occupancy, development approvals, offtake agreements, and references from institutional partners. Site visits available.
?
"This sounds too good to be true."
"The cash refund is funded from the bulk offtake discount our developer extends to us for bringing 10+ buyers at once". The appliance grant comes out of the same pool.
